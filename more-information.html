Goals

Our goal is to send supplies that will motivate and promote educational quality for the future of our Nepali community back home. Through this initiative, we aim to assist countless children in accomplishing everyday tasks that we may often take for granted but would be immensely appreciated by them.

What we need

We are seeking support from the entire Nepali diaspora in Texas and the DFW area. Donations and fundraising are vital components of our mission. Any contributions will help us achieve our objectives and make a positive difference. So, we encourage everyone to join us in this cause.

Why?

Most of us often complain about having to go to school and having a lot of work & reading to do. However, it's important to realize that not everyone has access to educational resources that we take for granted. For example, some children in Nepal don't have access to textbooks. A glimpse of a textbook would be considered as a luxury for them. Recognizing our privilage in having access to basic resources should motivate us to take action. By your support we can create a meaningful difference in providing access to education for all.
